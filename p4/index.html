Instructions

Step 1:

Typically a two-person game, but you can play it solo!

Step 2:

Click on one box, any box that isn't taken by your opponent, during each of your turns!

Step 3:

Get your designated pattern into a row of three, be it: vertically, horizontally, or diagonally to win!
